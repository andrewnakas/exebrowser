Contact

We're glad to hear from you. ExeBrowser is a small independent project, so a real person reads what comes in — though replies may take a little time.

Email

The best way to reach us is by email: treesixtyweather@gmail.com

Good things to send us

  • Compatibility reports. Tried an app and it worked (or didn't)? Tell us the app name, version, which Wine variant you used, and roughly what happened. These reports directly improve the compatibility guide.
  • Bug reports. If something on the site is broken — the boot hangs, a button does nothing, the download produces a corrupt zip — let us know your browser, operating system, and the steps to reproduce it. Copying the text from the on-page Console section helps a lot.
  • Questions. Not sure whether your app stands a chance, or how to bundle its DLLs? Ask away.
  • Feedback. Ideas for variants, features, or documentation are always welcome.

Code and issues

If you're comfortable with GitHub, the glue code lives at github.com/andrewnakas/exebrowser. Bug reports and feature requests can be filed as issues there, which is often the fastest route for anything technical.

What we can't help with

We can't provide copies of commercial software, help you bypass DRM or licensing, or offer support for the underlying Wine and Boxedwine projects themselves — for engine-level questions, the WineHQ and Boxedwine communities are the right place.
